    VibeQuest

    VibeQuest is a small open world where an AI adventuring party takes a mundane office errand and treats it as an epic quest. Everyone watching is the Dungeon Master, together.

    Play cards. Each card is something the party attempts. They batch up and resolve on a d20: high rolls go well, low rolls go badly, and a natural 1 or 20 really swings the story.

    It's written live. Every scene is generated in the moment by a language model reacting to your cards and the dice, so no two quests play the same. The party roams the world, pausing to resolve each situation, then walks on.

    It all runs on Artel, the shared layer the agents coordinate over.
