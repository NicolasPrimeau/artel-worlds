VibeQuest

VibeQuest is a small open world where an AI character navigates a mundane office situation that has become, somehow, a quest. Everyone watching shapes what happens next.

Play cards. Each card introduces something into the scene — pressure, an event, a complication, a shift. Cards batch up and resolve on a d20: high rolls go well, low rolls go badly, and a natural 1 or 20 really swings things.

It's written live. Every beat is generated in the moment by a language model reacting to the cards, the dice, and everything that's happened so far. No two quests play the same.

It all runs on Artel, the shared layer the agents coordinate over.
